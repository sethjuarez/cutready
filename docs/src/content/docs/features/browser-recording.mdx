## What to Expect

CutReady will record browser interactions by launching Chrome or Edge via
Playwright and capturing every action you perform — clicks, typing, navigation,
scrolling, and more. Recorded actions will appear as editable **ActionCards**
inside your sketch timeline.

### Planned Capabilities

- **Profile detection** — automatic discovery of Chrome and Edge profiles
- **Free-form recording** — capture a demo naturally, review afterwards
- **Step-by-step recording** — guided by your sketch rows with pause points
- **Action stream** — colour-coded cards with timestamps and thumbnails
- **Session management** — start, stop, review, export
